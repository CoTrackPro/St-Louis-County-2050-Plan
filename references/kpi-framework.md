Comprehensive KPI definitions organized by department. Each KPI includes
definition, data source, collection frequency, target-setting methodology,
and equity disaggregation notes.

## 1. Permits & Licensing

### 1.1 Average Permit Processing Time
- **Definition**: Calendar days from complete application submission to final decision (approved, denied, or returned for revision).
- **Data source**: Permit management system (date stamps on submission and decision events).
- **Frequency**: Monthly, reported as rolling 3-month average.
- **Target methodology**: Peer county median (ICMA) adjusted for application complexity mix. Segment by permit type: residential simple (target ≤5 days), residential complex (≤15 days), commercial (≤30 days).
- **Equity note**: Disaggregate by geographic zone (north/south/west/central) to detect service disparity.

### 1.2 Online Submission Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of permit applications submitted through digital portal vs. walk-in/mail.
- **Data source**: Portal analytics + manual intake logs.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: 85% by end of FY2027 (current baseline ~65%).
- **Equity note**: Track by applicant zip code. Low digital adoption in specific areas may signal need for outreach or assisted filing.

### 1.3 First-Pass Approval Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of applications approved without requiring resubmission or additional information.
- **Data source**: Permit system status transitions.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: 80%. Below 70% suggests unclear requirements or insufficient pre-application guidance.

### 1.4 Inspection Completion Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of scheduled inspections completed on the scheduled date.
- **Data source**: Inspector scheduling system.
- **Frequency**: Weekly (reported monthly).
- **Target**: 95%.

### 1.5 Permit Revenue Per FTE
- **Definition**: Total permit fee revenue divided by number of full-time equivalent staff in the permits division.
- **Data source**: Finance system + HR headcount.
- **Frequency**: Quarterly.
- **Target**: Benchmark against ICMA peer median.

### 1.6 Pending Application Backlog
- **Definition**: Count of applications in queue >30 days without a decision.
- **Data source**: Permit system aged report.
- **Frequency**: Weekly.
- **Target**: <5% of total active applications.
- **Alert threshold**: >10% triggers root cause review.

## 2. Revenue & Taxation

### 2.1 Property Tax Collection Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of levied property tax collected within the fiscal year.
- **Data source**: Tax collection system year-end reconciliation.
- **Frequency**: Annually (interim quarterly tracking).
- **Target**: 98%+ (national median for counties >500K pop is ~96.5%).

### 2.2 Online Payment Adoption
- **Definition**: Percentage of tax payments made via online portal vs. mail/walk-in.
- **Data source**: Payment gateway + cashier logs.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: 85% by FY2027.

### 2.3 Average Call Wait Time
- **Definition**: Mean hold time in minutes for Revenue Department phone line.
- **Data source**: Phone system ACD reports.
- **Frequency**: Daily (reported weekly).
- **Target**: ≤5 minutes. Peak season (Dec–Jan) may exceed; track separately.

### 2.4 Personal Property Declaration Compliance
- **Definition**: Percentage of required declarations filed by deadline.
- **Data source**: Declaration tracking system.
- **Frequency**: Annually.
- **Target**: 92%.

### 2.5 Senior Property Tax Freeze Applications Processed
- **Definition**: Count of Senior Tax Freeze applications received and processed to completion within 30 days.
- **Data source**: Application tracking.
- **Frequency**: Monthly during open period.
- **Target**: Process 100% within 30 days of receipt.

### 2.6 Assessment Appeal Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of property assessments formally appealed.
- **Data source**: Board of Equalization records.
- **Frequency**: Annually.
- **Target**: <3% (high appeal rate suggests assessment quality issues).

## 3. Parks & Recreation

### 3.1 Park Attendance
- **Definition**: Estimated total visits to county parks and recreation facilities.
- **Data source**: Trail counters, facility sign-ins, program registrations, parking counts.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: 5% year-over-year growth.

### 3.2 Program Fill Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of available program slots filled at session start.
- **Data source**: Registration system.
- **Frequency**: Per session (reported quarterly).
- **Target**: 85%. Below 70% triggers program review.

### 3.3 Maintenance Work Order Completion
- **Definition**: Percentage of maintenance work orders completed within SLA (routine: 7 days, urgent: 24 hours, emergency: 4 hours).
- **Data source**: Work order management system.
- **Frequency**: Weekly.
- **Target**: 90% within SLA.

### 3.4 Visitor Satisfaction Score
- **Definition**: Mean score on 5-point satisfaction survey administered at facilities and post-program.
- **Data source**: Survey platform (intercept + email follow-up).
- **Frequency**: Quarterly.
- **Target**: 4.3/5.0.
- **Equity note**: Disaggregate by facility location and respondent demographics.

### 3.5 Facility Utilization Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of bookable facility hours that are reserved/used.
- **Data source**: Reservation system.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: 75% for indoor facilities, 60% for outdoor.

### 3.6 Trail Condition Index
- **Definition**: Composite score based on annual trail condition assessments (surface quality, signage, safety features).
- **Data source**: Annual field inspection.
- **Frequency**: Annually.
- **Target**: 80/100.

## 4. Animal Services

### 4.1 Live Release Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of animals leaving the shelter alive (adoption + return-to-owner + transfer).
- **Data source**: Shelter management system (Chameleon or equivalent).
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: 90%+ (Best Friends Animal Society benchmark).

### 4.2 Average Length of Stay
- **Definition**: Mean calendar days from intake to outcome for all animals.
- **Data source**: Shelter management system.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: ≤14 days for dogs, ≤10 days for cats.

### 4.3 Adoption Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of outcomes that are adoptions.
- **Data source**: Shelter management system.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: 70%+.

### 4.4 Return-to-Owner Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of stray intakes reunited with owners.
- **Data source**: Shelter management system.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: 40%+ (driven by microchip scanning and lost-pet database integration).

### 4.5 Spay/Neuter Compliance
- **Definition**: Percentage of adopted animals spayed/neutered before or within 30 days of adoption.
- **Data source**: Shelter management system + follow-up tracking.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: 100%.

### 4.6 Field Response Time
- **Definition**: Average time from animal complaint call to officer arrival.
- **Data source**: Dispatch system.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: ≤60 minutes for urgent, ≤24 hours for routine.

## 5. Vital Records

### 5.1 Turnaround Time — Birth/Death Certificates
- **Definition**: Business days from request to fulfillment (mail or walk-in).
- **Data source**: Records management system.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: Walk-in same day; mail ≤5 business days.

### 5.2 Online Request Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of vital record requests submitted online.
- **Data source**: Online portal analytics.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: 60%.

### 5.3 Error Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of issued documents requiring correction/reissue.
- **Data source**: Correction log.
- **Frequency**: Quarterly.
- **Target**: <1%.

### 5.4 Customer Satisfaction Score
- **Definition**: Post-service satisfaction rating for vital records requests.
- **Data source**: Post-transaction survey (kiosk or email).
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: 4.2/5.0.

### 5.5 Request Volume Per FTE
- **Definition**: Total requests processed divided by FTE staff count.
- **Data source**: Records system + HR headcount.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: Benchmark against ICMA peers; track for capacity planning.

### 5.6 Same-Day Walk-In Completion Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of walk-in requests completed same visit (not requiring return or mail follow-up).
- **Data source**: Records system transaction status.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: 95%. Below 90% signals system issues or documentation gaps.

## 6. Board of Elections

### 6.1 Voter Registration Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of eligible residents registered to vote.
- **Data source**: Voter file vs. Census ACS estimates.
- **Frequency**: Updated each election cycle.
- **Target**: 90%+.

### 6.2 Voter Turnout
- **Definition**: Percentage of registered voters casting ballots.
- **Data source**: Certified election results.
- **Frequency**: Per election.
- **Target**: Varies by election type. Presidential >70%, municipal >30%.

### 6.3 Provisional Ballot Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of ballots cast as provisional.
- **Data source**: Election results.
- **Frequency**: Per election.
- **Target**: <3% (high rate suggests registration or communication issues).

### 6.4 Poll Worker Recruitment Fill Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of poll worker slots filled by election day.
- **Data source**: Poll worker management system.
- **Frequency**: Per election.
- **Target**: 100%.

## 7. Customer Service

### 7.1 Qless Virtual Queue Wait Time
- **Definition**: Average minutes from joining Qless virtual queue to being served.
- **Data source**: Qless analytics.
- **Frequency**: Daily (reported weekly).
- **Target**: ≤20 minutes.
- **Note**: Effective March 2025, in-person service is Mon–Thu 8am–3:30pm only. West County Government Center is closed. Monitor impact on remaining locations.

### 7.2 First-Contact Resolution Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of inquiries resolved without transfer, callback, or follow-up visit.
- **Data source**: CRM/ticketing system.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: 75%.

### 7.3 Digital Self-Service Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of constituent interactions completed through website, portal, or app without staff assistance.
- **Data source**: Web analytics + service transaction logs.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: 50% by FY2027.

### 7.4 Customer Satisfaction (CSAT)
- **Definition**: Post-interaction satisfaction score (5-point scale).
- **Data source**: Post-visit survey (SMS/email/kiosk).
- **Frequency**: Continuous (reported monthly).
- **Target**: 4.0/5.0.

### 7.5 Channel Distribution
- **Definition**: Breakdown of interactions by channel (walk-in, phone, email, web, Qless).
- **Data source**: Multi-channel CRM.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: Shift toward digital channels; reduce walk-in share by 10 pts/year.

## 8. Procurement

### 8.1 Average Procurement Cycle Time
- **Definition**: Calendar days from requisition to contract execution.
- **Data source**: Procurement system timestamps.
- **Frequency**: Quarterly.
- **Target**: ≤60 days for standard, ≤120 days for complex/construction.

### 8.2 Bid Response Rate
- **Definition**: Average number of responsive bids per solicitation.
- **Data source**: Bid management system.
- **Frequency**: Quarterly.
- **Target**: ≥3 responsive bids per solicitation.

### 8.3 Small/Minority/Women-Owned Business Participation
- **Definition**: Percentage of contract dollars awarded to certified S/M/WBE firms.
- **Data source**: Vendor registration + contract awards.
- **Frequency**: Quarterly.
- **Target**: Per county ordinance goals (typically 20–25%).
- **Equity note**: Critical equity KPI. Report by business category and geographic location.

### 8.4 Contract Compliance Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of active contracts with current insurance, bonding, and reporting requirements met.
- **Data source**: Contract management system.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: 95%.

## 9. Planning & Zoning

### 9.1 Zoning Application Processing Time
- **Definition**: Calendar days from complete application to hearing/decision.
- **Data source**: Planning case management system.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: ≤45 days for variances, ≤90 days for rezonings.

### 9.2 Comprehensive Plan Implementation Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of comprehensive plan action items completed or in progress.
- **Data source**: Annual comprehensive plan status report.
- **Frequency**: Annually.
- **Target**: 70% in progress or complete by plan midpoint.

### 9.3 GIS Data Currency
- **Definition**: Percentage of GIS layers updated within their scheduled refresh cycle.
- **Data source**: GIS data management log.
- **Frequency**: Quarterly.
- **Target**: 95%.

### 9.4 Pre-Application Meeting Availability
- **Definition**: Calendar days from request to earliest available pre-application meeting slot.
- **Data source**: Scheduling system.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: ≤10 business days. Long waits delay the entire permit journey.
- **Journey note**: This is the first touchpoint in the Homeowner Building Project journey. Delays here cascade.

### 9.5 Variance/Rezoning Approval Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of variance and rezoning applications approved (with or without conditions).
- **Data source**: Planning case management system.
- **Frequency**: Quarterly.
- **Target**: Track only (no target — high or low rate isn't inherently good/bad). Use for workload forecasting and to detect whether application guidance is working (high denial rate may signal unclear pre-application process).

### 9.6 Code Enforcement Complaint Response Time
- **Definition**: Business days from code enforcement complaint receipt to initial investigation.
- **Data source**: Complaint tracking system.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: ≤10 business days for routine, ≤3 for health/safety.
- **Equity note**: Disaggregate by zone. Inconsistent response times across areas signal resource allocation issues.

- **Data source**: Environmental health inspection system.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: ≤180 days (twice annually).

### 10.2 Immunization Coverage Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of county children ages 0–6 with up-to-date vaccinations.
- **Data source**: ShowMeVax (Missouri immunization registry).
- **Frequency**: Quarterly.
- **Target**: 90%+.

### 10.3 WIC Participation Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of eligible residents enrolled in WIC.
- **Data source**: WIC management system vs. Census eligibility estimates.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: 65%+ (national average ~57%).

### 10.4 Environmental Complaint Response Time
- **Definition**: Business days from complaint receipt to initial investigation.
- **Data source**: Complaint tracking system.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: ≤5 business days.

## 11. Transportation

### 11.1 Pothole Repair Time
- **Definition**: Calendar days from report to repair completion.
- **Data source**: 311/work order system.
- **Frequency**: Weekly (reported monthly).
- **Target**: ≤7 days routine, ≤48 hours for safety hazards.

### 11.2 Road Condition Index (PCI)
- **Definition**: Pavement Condition Index on 0–100 scale for county-maintained roads.
- **Data source**: Biennial pavement assessment.
- **Frequency**: Biennial.
- **Target**: Average PCI ≥70 (Good condition).

### 11.3 Signal Maintenance Response Time
- **Definition**: Hours from signal malfunction report to repair.
- **Data source**: Signal maintenance log.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: ≤4 hours.

### 11.4 Snow Route Clearance Time
- **Definition**: Hours from snowfall end to primary routes cleared.
- **Data source**: Fleet GPS + operations log.
- **Frequency**: Per event.
- **Target**: ≤12 hours for primary, ≤24 hours for secondary.

## 12. Police Department

### 12.1 Priority 1 Response Time
- **Definition**: Minutes from dispatch to officer arrival for life-threatening calls.
- **Data source**: CAD system.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: ≤8 minutes (90th percentile).

### 12.2 Community Engagement Events
- **Definition**: Count of organized community engagement activities (town halls, school visits, neighborhood meetings).
- **Data source**: Community affairs log.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: ≥10 per month per precinct.

### 12.3 Use-of-Force Incident Rate
- **Definition**: Use-of-force incidents per 1,000 arrests.
- **Data source**: Internal affairs / use-of-force database.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: Declining trend; benchmark against CALEA peer agencies.
- **Equity note**: Disaggregate by race/ethnicity of subject. Report alongside departmental demographic composition.

### 12.4 Case Clearance Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of reported crimes cleared by arrest or exceptional means.
- **Data source**: Records management system (UCR/NIBRS).
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: At or above national median by crime category.

## 13. Justice Services

### 13.1 Pretrial Supervision Compliance Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of pretrial supervisees meeting all conditions.
- **Data source**: Case management system.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: 85%.

### 13.2 Diversion Program Completion Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of diversion participants who successfully complete the program.
- **Data source**: Diversion tracking system.
- **Frequency**: Quarterly.
- **Target**: 75%.

### 13.3 Recidivism Rate (1-Year)
- **Definition**: Percentage of individuals rearrested within 12 months of supervision end.
- **Data source**: State criminal history check (MSHP).
- **Frequency**: Annually (cohort-based).
- **Target**: Below state average; declining trend.
- **Equity note**: Disaggregate by race, age, and program type.

### 13.4 Risk Assessment Completion Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of new supervisees who receive a validated risk/needs assessment (ORAS, LSI-R, or equivalent) within 14 days of assignment.
- **Data source**: Case management system.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: 95%. Assessment drives supervision level — without it, over/under-supervision occurs.

### 13.5 Average Caseload Per Officer
- **Definition**: Active supervisees per supervision officer, segmented by risk level.
- **Data source**: Case management system + HR headcount.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: ≤50 for standard, ≤25 for high-risk (APPA guidelines). Above target signals staffing need.

### 13.6 Reentry Service Connection Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of individuals completing supervision who are connected to at least 2 community services (housing, employment, behavioral health) at case closure.
- **Data source**: Case management system referral tracking.
- **Frequency**: Quarterly.
- **Target**: 60%.
- **Journey note**: This connects to the Justice-Involved Reentry journey map. Low connection rate predicts higher recidivism.

## 14. Human Services

### 14.1 CDBG Funds Timeliness
- **Definition**: Percentage of Community Development Block Grant funds expended within HUD-required timeframe.
- **Data source**: IDIS (HUD reporting system).
- **Frequency**: Quarterly.
- **Target**: Expenditure ratio ≤1.5.

### 14.2 Homeless Services — Housing Placement Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of individuals in emergency/transitional housing placed in permanent housing within 90 days.
- **Data source**: HMIS.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: 40%.

### 14.3 Senior Services Participation
- **Definition**: Unduplicated count of seniors (65+) served through county aging programs.
- **Data source**: Program enrollment tracking.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: Year-over-year growth of 5%.

### 14.4 Benefit Application Processing Time
- **Definition**: Business days from application to eligibility determination for county-administered benefits.
- **Data source**: Benefits management system.
- **Frequency**: Monthly.
- **Target**: ≤10 business days.

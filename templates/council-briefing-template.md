or County Council. Plain language, data-driven, action-oriented.

## Completion Instructions

1. Pull latest scorecard data from each department.
2. Select one headline metric that tells the period's story.
3. Highlight 3 wins (data-backed, specific).
4. Detail 2–3 areas requiring attention with root cause and action plan.
5. List recommended actions requiring Council awareness or approval.
6. Include constituent feedback if survey data is available.
7. Keep total length to 2 pages when printed.

## Tone Guidelines
- Plain language — no jargon, no acronyms without definition
- Lead with outcomes for residents, not internal process details
- Be honest about challenges without being alarmist
- Quantify fiscal impact of recommendations
- Acknowledge staff effort behind improvements
